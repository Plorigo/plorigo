# Maintainers

This file lists the people responsible for reviewing and merging changes to
Plorigo. It complements:

- [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) — how decisions are made and how roles work.
- [.github/CODEOWNERS](.github/CODEOWNERS) — which areas of the codebase each
  maintainer reviews (and where review is required before merge).

| Maintainer    | GitHub                                            | Role            | Areas     |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------- | --------- |
| Ismat Babirli | [@ismatBabirli](https://github.com/ismatBabirli)  | Lead maintainer | All areas |

The lead maintainer has final say on project direction, releases, and security
decisions, as described in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge pull requests in their areas (see
  [CODEOWNERS](.github/CODEOWNERS)).
- Triage issues and discussions.
- Uphold the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and act on reports under the
  [security policy](SECURITY.md).
- Ensure contributions follow [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md), including the
  [Contributor License Agreement](CLA.md).

## Becoming a maintainer

Plorigo is currently founder-led and early-stage. There is no fixed checklist,
but maintainership is offered to contributors with a sustained track record of
high-quality contributions and reviews who share the project's goals. See
[GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) for the full path.

The best way to start is to contribute and take part in
[Discussions](https://github.com/Plorigo/plorigo/discussions).

## Security contact

To report a vulnerability, **do not open a public issue.** Follow the
[security policy](SECURITY.md) (GitHub private vulnerability reporting or the
contact listed there).
